ABOUT M.R. Samarth Educare

M.R. Samarth Educare is the best coaching institute for NEET, IIT JEE MHT-CET and NDA. We also conduct standard 8th to 10th SSC & CBSE , class 11th science classes, class 12th science classes.

M.R. Samarth Educare was initiated in 2012 in the super vision & guidance of late Sh. M.R. Gupta with a goal to create a group which is Samarth enough to make a student Samarth by educating him. In the last five years we gained a lot of experience & got expertise with the help of great leaders of the Indian education system. Now we are Samarth (able) enough to provide a highly sophisticated & scientific education system which can improve our students in all aspects of life. M.R. Samarth Educareis working on a vision to curb the information asymmetry amongst students in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities. The idea is to give students a launch pad who are talented but lack exposure and mentorship when it comes to competitive exams.
